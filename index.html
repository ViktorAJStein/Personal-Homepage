I am a PhD candidate in the Applied Mathematics Group led by Gabriele Steidl at TU Berlin. I wrote my master's thesis entitle "Wasserstein gradient flows with an eye towards positive matrix measures" about sparse convex optimisation in spaces of measures.

When I am not doing maths, you can find me visiting the opera or the concert hall or playing badminton and table tennis.
